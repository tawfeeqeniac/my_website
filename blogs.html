Deep Work by Cal Newport

I would like to share the key ideas that I found important in this book. The reason I chose to read it is because it explores the philosophy of deep work — the ability to focus intensely on cognitively demanding tasks — and how modern digital tools often act as barriers to achieving that focus. The author argues that deep work not only enhances productivity and learning but can also lead to a flow state, where one becomes fully immersed and deeply satisfied in the process of work itself.
